Fill in your details on the form above, watch the preview update as you type, and download a clean PDF you can email straight to your client. No signup, no account, and no watermark.

How to make an invoice

  1. Add your business details. Enter your name or business name, email, and address in the "From" section.
  2. Add your client's details. Enter who the invoice is addressed to in "Bill to" — name, company, and address.
  3. Set the invoice number and dates. Pick an invoice number you can track later, plus the issue date and payment due date.
  4. List what you're billing for. Add one line per item or service, with a quantity and rate — the amount and total calculate automatically.
  5. Add tax or a discount if needed. Enter a percentage and it's applied to the total.
  6. Download your PDF. Click "Download PDF" and the finished invoice saves to your device, ready to email.

Frequently asked questions

Is this invoice generator really free?

Yes. There's no signup, no account, and no limit on how many invoices you can create. The PDF download has no watermark.

Where is my data stored?

Nowhere but your browser. Everything you type stays on your device — nothing is uploaded or saved on a server, so your client information stays private.

What should an invoice include?

At minimum: your business name and contact details, your client's name and address, a unique invoice number, the issue date and due date, a description of the work with quantities and rates, the total amount due, and your preferred payment method or terms.

How do I number my invoices?

A simple sequential system works well — for example INV-0001, INV-0002, and so on. Some people prefix with the year, like INV-2026-001, which makes it easier to organize invoices by year for taxes.

Can I use this for international clients?

Yes — choose your currency from the dropdown before downloading. The tool supports USD, EUR, GBP, INR, AUD, and CAD.

Can I save a template to reuse?

Not yet in the free version — each invoice starts fresh. A "save template" feature with your logo and saved client list is coming soon as part of InvoicePilot Pro.
